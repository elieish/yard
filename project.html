Projects
These structures embark on various enterprises in agricultural and other endeavours, as collectives. Co-operative enterprises include fresh produce farming, livestock farming, tourism & hospitality, building construction and retail, to name a few. By pulling together resources on both the private and public sectors and investing these in a sustainable manner, members of local enterprise structures generate employment and economic activity, where it is needed most.
Co-ops & Grass Roots,Structures' Projects
By stimulating investment and responsible participation along the value-add chain, local producers can extend their activities and broaden the inclusion of community members into other enterprises. In this way The YARDAgency is able to achieve its long-term ancillary objectives.
Special Projects
Special Projects undertaken aim to capacitate community-based youth-led enterprises in terms of their skill levels in conducting their core business activities, financial administration, networking, marketing and participation in opportunities for their success.
Local Fresh Produced Market
The establishment of the fresh produce markets throughout the country is a major boost for beneficiaries, who have a direct relationship with the markets and the very reason why these markets exist. YARDfacilitates the entrance of SMMEs into the market and their growth in that market.
